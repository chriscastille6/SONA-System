# Wave 1 instrument summary (IRB — approved scope)

**Study:** Goals as Reference Points and Risk Taking (`goals-refs`)

**Approved participant experience (Wave 1):** After consent and brief demographics, each participant completes **exactly four** hypothetical vignettes from the compact pool in [`vignettes.json`](vignettes.json) (IDs **R1**, **R2**, **E1**, **E2**). Vignettes are shown in **random order**. Each vignette is presented in **one** randomly assigned **goal frame** (below goal **or** above goal), not both. **R1** and **R2** are replication items: binary choice between a sure option and a risky option. **E1** and **E2** are extension items: 7-point acceptability (or equivalent) judgment. The session includes 1–2 attention checks, optional email for a findings summary, and a debrief. Expected duration: **15–25 minutes**.

**Relationship to other documents:** [`vignettes_with_predicted_patterns.html`](vignettes_with_predicted_patterns.html) includes **additional** replication and extension scenarios, priors, and predicted patterns for **design reference and future waves**. It is **not** part of Wave 1 unless explicitly added by amendment and reflected in the live survey.

**Live survey:** Configure Qualtrics (or equivalent) so Wave 1 implements **only** R1, R2, E1, E2 with the goal-frame randomization described above. See [`QUALTRICS_PARITY_CHECKLIST.md`](QUALTRICS_PARITY_CHECKLIST.md).
