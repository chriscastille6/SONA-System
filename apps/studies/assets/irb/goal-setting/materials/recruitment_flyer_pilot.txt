A Study in Decision Making

[Pilot flyer — participant-facing text; no cash compensation language.]

You are invited to participate in a pilot session for a research study. This study has been reviewed and received ethics clearance from the Human Subjects Institutional Review Board (HSIRB) at Nicholls State University (Protocol: IRB 2024-07-30-001 CBA). The study is being conducted by Dr. Christopher Castille (Associate Professor of Management, Primary Investigator), Dr. Ann-Marie R. Castille (Associate Professor of Management, Co-Investigator), Dr. Samantha Falgout (Assistant Professor of Accounting, Co-Investigator), and Dr. Adrien Maught (Assistant Professor of Marketing, Co-Investigator) from Nicholls State University. This study is part of a larger, multi-site research project.

Participation in this pilot session is voluntary and will take no more than 1 hour of your time. If you agree to participate, you will be asked to complete a decision-making task involving anagram puzzles (word puzzles) in a lab or classroom on the Nicholls State University campus. You will also be asked to complete brief questionnaires about the task, a personality measure, and basic demographics. By volunteering, you will have an opportunity to learn about research in organizational behavior and management and to help the research team refine study procedures before main data collection.

You may decide to withdraw from this study at any time by advising the researcher. Your identity in this study will be kept strictly confidential; your name will not be included or in any other way associated with the data collected in the study. All de-identified data will be securely stored on the Open Science Framework (OSF) and in secure digital locations.

The final decision about participation is yours. You may contact Dr. Christopher Castille (christopher.castille@nicholls.edu) if you have any questions or concerns about this study.
