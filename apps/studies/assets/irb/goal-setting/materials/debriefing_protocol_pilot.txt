Thank You for Your Participation

Thank you for taking part in our pilot study. Your time and effort help us prepare for our upcoming research. This letter explains the purpose of this pilot session, how your responses will be used, and what we are investigating.

Study Purpose and Calibration

The primary purpose of this session was to pilot our research materials and practice administration protocols. Specifically, we are measuring baseline student performance on the anagram task to set an empirical 90th percentile target goal for the subsequent main study. This calibration ensures that the goals we assign in the main study are challenging but realistic for our student population.

There are no experimental manipulations, hidden conditions, or partial disclosures in this pilot study. All participants were asked to do their best to create as many words as possible.

How Your Responses Were Used

During the study, you completed anagram rounds in a workbook. All data collected in this pilot study are completely anonymous. Your name and email are never associated with your answers, workbooks, or self-reported scores. Data are stored securely using de-identified code IDs.

What We Are Investigating

This pilot study is a crucial preparatory step for a larger, multi-site research project coordinated through the Advancement of Replications Initiative in Management (ARIM). By practicing our administration protocols and calibrating our performance targets, we ensure the high quality and reliability of the data we will collect in the main study.

Questions or Concerns

If you have questions about the study or these procedures, please contact Dr. Christopher Castille (Principal Investigator) at christopher.castille@nicholls.edu.

This study has been reviewed and received ethics clearance through the Human Subjects Institutional Review Board (HSIRB) at Nicholls State University (Protocol: IRB 2024-07-30-001 CBA). If you have questions about your rights as a research participant, contact the HSIRB Chairperson, Dr. Alaina Daigle (985-448-4697, alaina.daigle@nicholls.edu), or your college HSIRB representative at Nicholls State University.

Summary of Findings

When data collection and analysis are complete, we will use the baseline performance data to set the targets for the main study. If you would like a summary of the aggregated findings from the overall project, please contact Dr. Christopher Castille at christopher.castille@nicholls.edu.

Thank you again for your assistance. We sincerely hope you found the session worthwhile.
