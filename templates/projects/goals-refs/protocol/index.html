Study protocol

Procedures

  1. Consent: You will read a short consent form and indicate that you agree to participate (18+).
  2. Vignette: You will read one scenario about a person (“Alex”) in an organization. The scenario describes the organization’s culture (e.g., scarce vs. shared resources) and how colleagues describe Alex (e.g., more or less willing to use manipulation).
  3. Ratings: You will rate how likely you think Alex would be to do each of several behaviors (1 = Extremely unlikely … 7 = Extremely likely). Items cover task focus, sharing information, production deviance, social undermining, sabotage, and supervisor appraisal.
  4. Debriefing: You will see a short explanation of the study’s purpose.

Duration

About 5–10 minutes.

Note: There are no course credit opportunities with this study.
