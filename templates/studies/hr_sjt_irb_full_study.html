Participant flow (for protocol text)

  1. Consent → ID from name (live app) → instructions → 27 situations with 1–5 tactic ratings.
  2. Skip / optional ratings: Each situation below has Skip this situation. Rating radios have no required attributes — participants may leave any or all ratings blank.
  3. Optional decision notes: Each situation ends with an optional free-text box so students can describe anything they would like the instructor to know about how they are thinking through that decision.
  4. Not forced choice: There is no forced ranking and no requirement to select an effectiveness rating before continuing.
IRB check: Reviewers can confirm participants are not forced to answer every situation by (1) leaving ratings blank and (2) using Skip this situation on any card below.
